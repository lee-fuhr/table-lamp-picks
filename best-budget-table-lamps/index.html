Quick answer

The JOOFO Touch Control Table Lamp is our top budget pick for its combination of touch dimmer, USB port, and solid build at an unbeatable price.

The Lepower offers a handy memory function for those who want consistent brightness, while the AMIR is the go-to choice for small nightstands thanks to its compact footprint.

A bedside table lamp does more than just light up a room, it sets the mood for winding down, provides focused light for reading, and often serves as a charging hub for your phone. You don’t need to spend a fortune to get a reliable, feature-rich lamp; today’s budget-friendly options include touch controls, adjustable brightness, and even USB ports. The trick is knowing which extra features genuinely improve your nightly routine and which are just nice-to-haves. When shopping on a tight budget, prioritize a lamp that offers at least two or three brightness levels (a 3-way dimmer is ideal) and a stable base. Touch controls are common in this price range, but pay attention to user reviews about sensitivity and reliability. Also consider size, if your nightstand is small, a compact design matters more than a larger base. Finally, a built-in USB port is a major bonus if you tend to charge your phone overnight, but verify that the output is sufficient for fast charging.
