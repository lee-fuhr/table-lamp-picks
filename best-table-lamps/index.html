Touch control table lamps have become a bedside staple. They free you from fumbling for a switch in the dark, and most now include USB ports for charging your phone or e-reader right on the nightstand. Dimming options let you shift from bright reading light to a soft glow without leaving bed. When choosing one, focus on brightness range, number of dimmer levels, and whether the lamp remembers your last setting. Size and style matter too, a lamp that’s too tall or too short can throw off your whole bedside setup. USB charging is handy, but check whether the port placement makes sense for your cords. Best for specific needs

Best for bedside reading

If you read in bed regularly, the Brightech Light PRO is ideal because of its five brightness levels and adjustable color temperature. You can dial in a warm amber for relaxing or a cool white for focusing on small text. The extra light control makes it easier on the eyes compared to fixed-temperature lamps. Our pick: Brightech Light PRO.

Best for small nightstands

The JOOFO Touch Control Lamp has a compact footprint that fits neatly on a cramped nightstand. Its simple design doesn’t protrude, leaving room for your phone, glasses, and a book. Best for those who hate resetting brightness

The Lepower Touch Control Lamp remembers your last dimmer setting, so you don’t have to tap through three levels every time you switch it on. It’s a small convenience that makes a big difference if you use the same low light night after night. The memory feature is rare in this price range, making Lepower a smart pick for habit-driven users. Our pick: Lepower Touch Control Lamp.

Our verdict

No single lamp fits every nightstand, but for most people the TaoTronics TT-TL032 offers the best mix of features, build quality, and price. If you can spend a little more and want adjustable color, go with the Brightech Light PRO. On a tighter budget, the JOOFO covers the essentials, while the Lepower adds a memory function that many will appreciate. Whichever you choose, a touch control lamp will make your bedside routine a little more pleasant.
